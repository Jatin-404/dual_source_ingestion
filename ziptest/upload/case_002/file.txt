This is a criminal case involving allegations of financial fraud under the Indian Penal Code.

The accused is charged with misappropriation of funds amounting to INR 1.2 crore from a private firm where he was employed as a financial manager.

According to the prosecution, the accused manipulated internal accounting systems to divert funds over a period of 18 months.

The defense contends that the discrepancies were due to accounting errors and denies intentional wrongdoing.

The case is currently being heard in the Mumbai Sessions Court.
