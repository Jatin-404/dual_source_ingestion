This case concerns a contractual dispute between ABC Pvt Ltd and XYZ Enterprises.

ABC Pvt Ltd alleges that XYZ Enterprises failed to deliver goods as per the agreed timeline specified in the contract dated June 2023. Despite multiple notices, the defendant did not fulfill the obligations.

The plaintiff is seeking damages amounting to INR 25,00,000 along with interest and legal costs.

The defendant argues that delays were caused due to unforeseen supply chain disruptions and denies liability.

The matter is currently under consideration before the Delhi High Court.
